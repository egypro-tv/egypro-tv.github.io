الوصف: Halûk and Emin lost their father at a young age. When their uncle İbrahim passed away, he left them his villa in a town in the Aegean region. Before selling the villa, Halûk and Emin go there with their girlfriends to enjoy a vacation. The brothers, who had fallen for two young women in the town, hurriedly sent their girlfriends back to the city to have an affair. However, they would pay a heavy price for their infidelity.
