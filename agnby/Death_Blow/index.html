Death Blow (2023)

⭐ 1.5 مناسب للعائلة

الوصف: After a poor real estate decision a family man, David finds himself in debt and can't find a way out. While checking on one of his rental properties he happens upon a bag containing a half a million dollars. Will David take the money and possibly put his family in serious danger or will he find another way out. Death Blow will lead you down a path with twist and turns but is it the path of the righteous or the path of evil?
