Night at the Museum: Battle of the Smithsonian (2009)

⭐ 6.2 مناسب للعائلة

الوصف: Hapless museum night watchman Larry Daley must help his living, breathing exhibit friends out of a pickle now that they've been transferred to the archives at the Smithsonian Institution. Larry's (mis)adventures this time include close encounters with Amelia Earhart, Abe Lincoln and Ivan the Terrible.
