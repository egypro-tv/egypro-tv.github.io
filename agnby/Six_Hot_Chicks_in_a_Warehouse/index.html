Six Hot Chicks in a Warehouse (2019)

⭐ 3.3 مناسب للعائلة

الوصف: Adrian, an insecure fashion photographer decides he has had enough rejection, ridicule and being ignored by models. He decides to get his revenge by concocting a photo-shoot with six of the models who abused him. However, in this photoshoot – it’s survival of the fittest as he pits girl against girl in more and more extreme scenarios until all of them are dead! Only both Adrian and the other models are in for a surprise…
