Semur 2: Cinlerin Büyüsü (2020)

⭐ 0.0 مناسب للعائلة

الوصف: Semur 2: The Magic of the Jinn tells the story of a group of friends who go to an ancient Greek village in search of things worth gold. The life of 25-year-old Greek-born Alesia is turned upside down by a letter from her grandfather. In the letter, his grandfather, Greeg, mentions things worth gold found in a Greek village. Deciding to follow what was written in the letter, Alesia goes to an old Greek village called Yorgalar with her friends. Young people face terrible events here. Here, Alesia is forced to face the terrible truths of her past.
